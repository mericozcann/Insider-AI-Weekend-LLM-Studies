# Insider-AI-Weekend-LLM-Studies

AI-Powered Financial Intelligence, Risk Analytics & Visual AI Pipelines  
Showcasing advanced AI systems developed and presented during Insider AI Weekend.

This repository contains a collection of end-to-end artificial intelligence pipelines focused on financial risk intelligence, fraud detection, large language models (LLMs), computer vision, prompt engineering, automation systems, and portfolio-level decision intelligence. All projects were developed and presented during the Insider AI Weekend program as a professional AI portfolio showcase.

### Hybrid Fraud Risk Scoring System
File:
- hybrid_fraud_risk_scoring_lstm_lightgbm_shap.py

A hybrid fraud detection and risk scoring architecture combining:
- LSTM for sequential transaction pattern recognition  
- LightGBM for structured risk classification  
- SHAP for explainable AI and model transparency  

The system produces interpretable, production-oriented fraud risk scores.
